About Us

OURS is a law firm with commitment not to do any different thing. We do the things differently.

We have a strong legal base in varied fields of legal activities in as much as all the legal team members of the company have sufficient experience in their respective fields having proficiency, professional acumen and knowledge in the areas such as Corporate Law (Company Law & Insolvency), Labour & Services, IPR, Arbitration, Debt Recovery, Criminal Law, Family Law, Commercial Law, Property Law, Consumer Law, Profile investigation at any pre contractual stage followed by Feasibility-cum-Due-Diligence report. The services include consultancy and Litigation support in the court of law apart from drafting of deed & documents.

We provide services to the various Govt. organizations, PSU and private companies of repute providing consultancy / advice, solicitation and litigation support in almost all the known fields of Law.

We have strong and reliable legal network to cater to the needs of our clients wherever they are based or established.

The unique feature of our firm is that it maintains confidentiality at one hand and transparency on the other hand by providing and updating information about the case status of its respective clients through dedicated and specially designed portal of the firm


CONSTITUTION OF THE FIRM

Mr. J.M. Kalia, Advocate is the founder promoter of the company having started his carrier at a very young age in the year 1985. He handled cases of varied nature and provided litigation support to the esteemed clients apart from issue based consultancy and solicitation. He was enrolled with Bar Council of Delhi in January, 1985.

A post graduate in law (LL.M) besides obtaining bachelor degree in B.Com and LL.B, (Delhi University) was awarded fellowship by the Institute of Constitutional & Parliamentary Studies, New Delhi. He served for a brief period as faculty member (Law faculty Delhi University) as also Guest faculty of Law Colleges under GGSIP University, Delhi. He has been guest faculty at Institute of Constitutional & Parliamentary Studies, Delhi (An Autonomous Institute under the Ministry of Parliament Affairs), Indian Academy of Highway Engineering (Ministry of Road and Transport, Govt. of India).

He has been legal advisor to the Govt. of NCT of Delhi in the Department of Revenue rendering day to day legal advice on various subjects including land, stamp duty, registration of instrument etc. He is also legal advisor to the Department of Information Technology (Govt. of NCT of Delhi) assisting the Adjudicating Officer (Secretary Information Technology) under the Information Technology Act in the adjudication of dispute relating to infringement of Cyber Laws, apart from rendering day to day advice in the related field of cyber laws. Including but not limited to, he:-

  • He assisted in the establishment and implementation of the regime of Law governing information and Technology in Delhi.
  • Drafted the bill governing Right of Citizen to have time bound delivery of citizen related services and personal liability of Govt. servant in case of any default and to pay compensatory cost to the citizen. The bill after having been approved by the cabinet was passed by the Delhi Assembly in the Assembly Session of March 2011 and notified under the title “Delhi (Right of Citizen to Time Bound Delivery of Services), Act, 2011”.
  • Drafted the bill pertaining to Governance, Management Security and safety of GEO Spatial Data created by the Delhi Government, which was also passed in the Delhi Assembly Session of March, 2011 and duly notified bearing title “Delhi Geo-Spatial Data Infrastructure (Management, Control Administration Safety, Security and Safety Act, 2011”.
  • Drafted the rules for state Government of Delhi under the Land Acquisition Act to facilitate time bound adjudication of legal proceeding and payment to the farmers though ECS Mode, as per court’s direction in writ petition titled Mahender Singh Versus Divisional Commissioner & Ors. Vide W.P.(C) No.395 of 2010.
  • Drafted guidelines for the Adjudicating Officers appointed under Information Technology Act, which was circulated to all adjudicating officers throughout the country as per the direction of Cyber Appellate Tribunal.
  • As member of the committee, drafted the Delhi (Court Fee) Amendment, Bill, passed and notified by the Govt. of NCT of Delhi effective 01-08-2012.
  • Appointed member of the 4th revision (minimum / circle rate) committee, Govt. of NCT of Delhi for revision of minimum / circle rate of immoveable properties as per the mandate under section 27 of the Indian Stamp Act read with Delhi Stamp (Prevention of under valuation of instruments) Rules, 2007 and submitted report thereof to the office of the Lt. Governor, Delhi.
  • Appointed by Govt. of NCT of Delhi as member secretary of committee to examine efficacy of existing money lending legislation in Delhi and submitted report recommending to regulate Micro Finance Business in Delhi including framing of rules under the Punjab Registration of Money Lending Act, 1938, as short time measures and framed new Bill / Act as long time measures in terms with model law based upon suggestions of the Govt. of India.
  • Represented the Delhi Govt. through Department of Information & Technology and participated in the meetings of the computer committee Delhi High Court for the E-governance of the litigation particularly online e- payment of court fee.
  • Appointed in 2012 by Govt. of NCT of Delhi as special counsel in Delhi High Court, in which constitutionality of the Court Fee (Delhi Amendment) Act, 2012 was questioned and assisted Senior Advocate Sh. Harish Salve in the aforesaid matter.
  • Appointed special counsel for Govt. of NCT of Delhi in AADHAR matter [W.P.(C) No.494 of 2012] pending in Supreme Court of India in which constitutionally of the regime pertaining to ADHAR was challenged.
