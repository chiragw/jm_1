Know Your Consumer Rights

adminNovember 1, 2016January 25, 2017 Blog No Comments

PROTECTION AGAINST

  1. Defective supply of goods,
  2. Sefective services,
  3. Manipulation of price of goods supplied or conditions of delivery or to affect flow of supplies in the market so as to impose unjustified cost or restriction on the consumers relating to goods and services.
  4. Unfair trade practices which is for the purpose of promoting sale use or supply of any goods or services, falsely representing the goods of a particular standard, quality, quantity, grade, composition, style or model……….

 

WHAT IS COMPLAINT?

  • ANY ALLEGATION MADE IN WRITING  BY A COMPLAINANT THAT ———
  • AN UNFAIR TRADE PRACTICE OR A RESTRICTIVE TRADE PRACTICE HAS BEEN ADOPTED BY ANY TRADER OR SERVICE PROVIDER.
  • SERVICES HIERED OR GOODS SUPPLIED SUFFERS FROM SOME DEFECTS.

RELIEF FOR WHICH CONSUMER IS ENTITLED TO

  1. to replacement of defective goods or equivalent price thereof,
  2. compensation against loss and injury suffered by consumer,
  3. to discontinue the unfair/restrictive trade practices,
  4. direction not to offer/manufacture hazardous goods for sale
  5. direction to neutralize the misleading advertisement.
